Hand and Fingers

No two individuals can have more than 8 similarities in fingerprint patterns. This allows us to identify people based on the curves and shape of your hands! Cool right?






















Retina and Iris

They say the eye is the window to the soul. Well... what if you can tell who someone is just by looking at their eye patterns? Introducing iris and retina scanning technologies! These can analyze and recognize patterns in your eye. Even blind people can use them. Contact us for more information.






















Your Gait

Everyone's walk is different! Sensors can recognize the pattern of your gait: where do you put the most pressure, or how fast do you go?






















Voice Recognition

Hello? Do you hear me? Well, guess what? Not only can this machine recognize your voice... it can also analyze it with a spectogram and find your secrets... (that was a joke)






















Vein Geometry

Fun Fact: You have unique veins. By analyzing the bright and dark lines and patterns of your veins, vein geometry can be used to identify different people.
